About

Design executive with agency roots and SaaS ambition

I started my career shaping brands at agencies like Grey Group, McCann, and TBWA, working with clients from Nissan to Air Canada. That foundation in storytelling and craft followed me into the product world, where I've spent the last decade leading design and product teams at scale.

At Shopify, I redesigned the checkout that processes $17B+ in annual payments and helped take the company to IPO. At McAfee, I led product experience for a $1.4B consumer business across 5 time zones and 4 countries. At Lightspeed, I shipped a product suite that grew POS revenue by 43%. Today, I'm reimagining travel and expense at Emburse with AI-first design.

I build teams that ship. I care about the craft, the culture, and the commercial outcome in equal measure.

Leadership

What I believe in

Empowering Autonomy

Trusting the UX team to take ownership of their work, while providing the support they need to thrive and go fast.

Cross-Team Collaboration

Building bridges between design, product, and engineering by default. One brain with three different perspectives to define the problem discovery and solution space.

Cultivating a Learning Culture

Encouraging continuous skill growth, curiosity, and experimentation — so we're always leveling up our UX game.

Beyond the Screen

Things with soul and emotion in their movement

I believe great design doesn't stop at pixels. It lives in the weight of a well-made door handle, the timing of a perfectly tuned engine, the moment a guest walks into a space and feels immediately at home.

I'm an Airbnb host who obsesses over the guest experience the same way I obsess over a user journey. I host a local farmers market on our farm in the mountains. In my free time, I restore cars, build furniture, design engines, and tinker with electronics.

Whether it's a SaaS product or a handmade table, I care about how things work, how they feel, and whether they were made with intention.
